To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.\n2.Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack, then stir through the meat mixture.\n3.Pour over 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce recipe and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season.\n4.Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.\n5.Heat oven to 180C/fan/160C/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm)\n6.Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made or homemade white sauce\n7.Repeat until you have 3 layers of pasta.
